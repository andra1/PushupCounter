How it works

The Ring

The form quality ring is the visual centerpiece — like Strava's route map. It animates from 0% to the actual score when the card appears. Color is green/cyan for good form, shifts toward amber/red for poor form.

The Stats

Three key numbers: total pushups, number of sets, total active time across all sessions that day.

Per-Set Chips

Small chips at the bottom show the count per set. Color reflects that set's form quality (green = good, amber = okay, red = poor).

Sharing

In-app: this card appears on the Today view and in History. Share button exports a short animated video (the ring filling up + stats appearing) or a static image.
